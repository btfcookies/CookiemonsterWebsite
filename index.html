My Journey

2023

Started Programming

I learned my first language, Python, in a middle school computer science class. I experimented with Pygame to create my first simple 2D games.

2024

Founded Eightbit Games

I founded Eightbit Games in, the game development studio that has helped me bring my game ideas to life for 4 years.

2024

Began development of BTF OG Edition

The same year that I founded Eightbit Games, I started working on BTF OG Edition. BTF OG Edition is a browser RNG game that I stopped development on a in 2025.

2025

Began development on BTF Vibe Edition

After taking a little break from coding over the summer, I began working on a new and improved version of BTF, which was my first major success.

2026

Development stops on BTF Vibe Edition

Around January of 2026, I decided that Javascript just wasn't the best language for making games. I decided to stop working on BTF Vibe Edition and start working on a Unity version of BTF (BTF 2)

2026

Current projects

I since January, I have mainly been working on BTF 2. I have also been working on ProtoReader, a quiz bowl practice website that is sort of a side project.
