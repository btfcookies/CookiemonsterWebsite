About Me

Hello there! I'm Cookiemonster, a full stack developer trying to make an impact in the world of technology.

I used to work mainly with Unity and Roblox Studio to make games, but I've since shifted my focus to machine learning and web development.

My current projects include Zep, a cutting edge Discord bot, and the Titanitron, a machine learning model for predicting Titanic survival.
